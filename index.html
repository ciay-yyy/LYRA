Who We Are

About LYRA

LYRA is a student-led fashion label born at Singapore Polytechnic, created by passionate Year 1 students who believe that style is a language everyone speaks. Named after the brightest star constellation, LYRA represents the intersection of creativity, culture, and contemporary fashion.

✨

Our Vision

To create accessible, culturally-rich fashion that celebrates Singapore's diverse heritage while embracing global trends. Every piece tells a story of identity and aspiration.

🎓

Student-Led

Designed, curated, and launched by Year 1 students at Singapore Polytechnic. LYRA is our collective first chapter — and we're just getting started.

🌏

Made for Everyone

We design for all sizes, all genders, and all cultures. Our collections are inclusive by design — because fashion should never have a gate at its door.

🟠

Orange Box Collab

All our tees are produced with Singapore Orange Box using premium DTF printing on dri-fit polyester — durable, vivid, and made to last through every wash.
